How to play Cacho (Dudo/Perudo)

Players secretly roll dice, then bid how many of a face exist across all dice. Next player must raise or call Dudo.

  • Aces wild (optional): ⚀ counts toward any non-⚀ bid.
  • Switch rule (Perudo): To ⚀ requires ≥ ceil(q/2). From ⚀ requires ≥ 2×q. (Simple: raise like normal.)
  • Dudo: Reveal all dice. If bid holds (actual ≥ bid), challenger loses a die; otherwise bidder loses one.
  • Elimination: Reach 0 dice → out. Loser of a die starts next round (if eliminated, next alive seat starts).
